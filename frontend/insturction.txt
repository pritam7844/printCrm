1. System Overview

PrintCRM is a fully custom web-based CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system designed specifically for a print-on-demand business that produces printed products such as cups, cards, visiting cards, banners, and similar items.

The system manages the complete lifecycle of a customer — from the first inquiry on WhatsApp or a Meta Ad, through order placement, production, delivery, and payment — all in one place, accessible by the entire team with role-based permissions.

4. Customer Management

When a lead places their first order, they become a saved Customer in the system. All their information, order history, notes, and payment records are stored permanently in their profile.

4.1 Customer Profile Contains
Full name, phone number, email, address
Total number of orders placed
Total amount spent (lifetime value)
Total outstanding balance (if any unpaid orders)
Date of first order and last order
Source — how they first came in (Meta Ad / WhatsApp / Walk-in etc.)

All notes ever added across all orders
Full activity history

Team searches customer by name or phone number

Payment and financial data is protected by role-based access:
Only Owner, Manager, and Accountant can view payment records
Only Owner and Accountant can access full financial reports
Sales Agents can record a payment but cannot view earnings reports
Production and Delivery staff have no access to payment data
Owner can customize any individual's payment permissions

8. Integrations — Meta Ads & WhatsApp

8.1 Meta Lead Ads Integration
When a customer sees an ad on Facebook or Instagram and fills in the instant lead form, their information automatically appears in the CRM within seconds — no manual work needed.

How It Works
Business runs a Lead Ad campaign on Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
Customer clicks ad and fills the instant form (name, phone, what they need)
Meta sends this data to the CRM via a secure Webhook (Meta Graph API)
CRM instantly creates a new lead with all customer details
Assigned team member receives a notification immediately
Lead appears in the CRM pipeline tagged as Source: Meta Ad

Data Captured from Meta Lead Ad
Customer full name
Phone number
Email address (if collected in form)
Which ad they came from (campaign name)
Date and time of form submission
Any custom questions added to the form (e.g. 'What do you want printed?')

Meta Lead Ads API is free to use. You only pay for the ad spend (cost per lead). Setup takes approximately 1-2 hours by a developer.

8.2 WhatsApp Business API Integration
When a customer sends a message on WhatsApp Business, an automated bot collects their details and a lead is instantly created in the CRM.
